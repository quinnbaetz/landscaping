## Conventions

- **BOC** = Backyard Orchard Culture (close-spaced fruit trees kept short by summer pruning).
- **Position codes:** `T1–T17` = freestanding trees (see main-lot-planting.md). Compass references: N/S/E/W relative to the lot's back-edge (north wall). True north is rotated ~26° clockwise from this.
- **9b** = USDA Zone 9b. **Sunset 14** = Sunset climate zone.
- Cap = imported topsoil layer over the structural clay fill.
- All measurements in US units (ft, in, gpm, psi, cu yd).
