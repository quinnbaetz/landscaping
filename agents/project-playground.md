# Project — Playground Area

## Intent

Shaded kid play space. **Kid-safe first** (no toxic, no thorns, no contact-rash plants). Edible where possible. Sensory (fragrance, texture, color).

## DO NOT plant near play area

Foxglove (*Digitalis*), oleander (*Nerium*), datura, hellebore, lily of the valley, monkshood, yew, castor bean, daffodil bulbs, daphne, rhubarb leaves. **Also avoid:** berberis, pyracantha, roses, any thorned bramble (eye-height spines).

## Plant palette

| Plant | Sci. | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Alpine strawberry 'Mignonette' | *Fragaria vesca* | Edible groundcover. Tiny intense berries kids pick. Shade-tolerant |
| Wild strawberry | *Fragaria chiloensis* | CA native. Runner-spreading. Edible berries |
| Chamomile | *Matricaria recutita* | Soft feathery cushion. Apple-scented daisy flowers. Edible |
| Sweet violet | *Viola odorata* | Edible scented flowers. Spreading groundcover |
| Lemon balm | *Melissa officinalis* | Lemon brush-and-smell. Edible/tea. **Confine in sunk pot** |
| Apple mint | *Mentha suaveolens* | Soft fuzzy apple-scented leaves. Kid-friendly mint. **Confine in sunk pot** |
| Heuchera (coral bells) | *Heuchera* | Wide foliage-color range. Safe. Hummingbird flowers |
| Hostas | *Hosta* | Big leaves to touch. Pick smaller cultivar ('Mouse Ears') if tight |
| Native sword fern | *Polystichum munitum* | Evergreen CA native. Tactile fronds |
| Wild ginger | *Asarum caudatum* | Glossy heart leaves. Safe. Faint ginger smell |
| Red currant | *Ribes rubrum* | **Thornless**. Shade-tolerant. Tart red berries mid-summer (3–4 ft) |
| Evergreen huckleberry | *Vaccinium ovatum* | Slow native. Small edible blue berries late summer |

## Layout

- Plants at **edges**, not middle (kids run through middle)
- Anchor shrubs (currant, huckleberry) at back/corners
- Mid-bed: heuchera, hostas, ferns
- Front edge: strawberries, chamomile, violets, wild ginger
- Mints/lemon balm in sunk 5-gal nursery pots (bottoms cut out)
- Mulch: 2 in soft fine chips/bark. **Avoid gorilla hair/shredded redwood** (sharp edges near where kids fall)
