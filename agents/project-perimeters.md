# Project — Perimeters

## Intent

Shaded perimeter beds. Low-growing perennials/herbs (under ~3 ft). Pollinator + pest-deterrent (esp. mosquito) + aromatic + culinary + medicinal.

## Conditions

Partial shade default (buildings cast shadows for big chunks of day).

## Plant palette

Tag legend: **P** = pollinator, **X** = pest/mosquito deterrent, **A** = aromatic, **C** = culinary, **M** = medicinal.

| Plant | Sci. | Tags | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Lemon balm | *Melissa officinalis* | X A C M | Mosquito repellent. Calming tea. Self-seeds — confine in sunk pot |
| Catmint 'Walker's Low' | *Nepeta* | P X A | Bee magnet. Long bloom May–Oct |
| Peppermint | *Mentha × piperita* | X A C M | Strongest mosquito-repelling mint. **Confine in sunk bottomless container** (spreads aggressively) |
| Sweet woodruff | *Galium odoratum* | A M | 6 in shade groundcover. Hay-vanilla scent dried |
| Wild ginger | *Asarum caudatum* | A C | CA native. Heart-shaped leaves. Faint ginger scent |
| Bee balm | *Monarda didyma* | P A C M | Hummingbird + bee. Bergamot tea. Tolerates partial shade |
| Sage 'Berggarten' | *Salvia officinalis* | A C M | Broad-leaf culinary. Tolerates half-shade. Under 2 ft |
| Sweet violet | *Viola odorata* | P A C | Fragrant edible flowers early spring. Self-seeds gently |
| Calendula | *Calendula officinalis* | P M | Edible petals. Salve/oil base. Long bloom, reseeds |
| Yarrow 'Cerise Queen' | *Achillea millefolium* | P M | Wound-healing. Survives partial shade |
| Chamomile | *Matricaria recutita* | P A C M | Apple-scented tea flowers. Self-seeds annually |
| Garlic chives | *Allium tuberosum* | P X C | Aphid deterrent. Mild garlic flavor. Late-summer bee flowers |
| Lungwort | *Pulmonaria officinalis* | P M | Early-spring bumblebee plant. Silver-spotted leaves. Pure shade |

## Layout

- Drifts of 3–5 of same species (NOT singletons)
- Tall (bee balm, catmint, sage) at back, mid (lemon balm, calendula, chamomile, garlic chives, yarrow) middle, groundcovers (sweet woodruff, wild ginger, sweet violet, lungwort) front
- **Confine runners** (mints, lemon balm, bee balm): sink a 5-gal nursery pot with bottom cut out
- Self-reseeders (calendula, chamomile, sweet violet) fill gaps year over year
- Mulch LIGHTLY (1–2 in fine compost/chips) — heavy mulch smothers low groundcovers
