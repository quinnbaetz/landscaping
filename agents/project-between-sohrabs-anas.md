# Project — Between Sohrab's & Ana's House

## Intent

Same screening intent as the Justin/Ana side. Different plant mix so the two side yards don't read as identical from inside. Cluster planting with 4–5 ft gaps for a walkable path.

## Conditions

Partial shade between two house walls. Push loquat + tea camellia to the shadier end; pomegranate + rosemary to the sunnier end.

## Plant palette

| Plant | Sci. | Mature size | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pineapple guava 'Apollo' | *Feijoa sellowiana* | 8–12 ft × 6–8 ft | Anchor. Evergreen. Self-fertile. Different cultivar from other side |
| Dwarf olive 'Little Ollie' | *Olea europaea* | 4–6 ft × 4–6 ft | Compact non-fruiting evergreen. Silver foliage contrast |
| Pomegranate (shrub form) | *Punica granatum* | 6–10 ft × 6–8 ft | Semi-evergreen. Multi-trunked. Edible fruit |
| Loquat 'Champagne' | *Eriobotrya japonica* | 10–15 ft (can prune smaller) | Evergreen. Bold leaves. Spring fruit. Shade-tolerant |
| Tea camellia | *Camellia sinensis* | 4–6 ft × 3–5 ft | Evergreen. Tea leaves |
| Upright rosemary 'Barbeque' | *Salvia rosmarinus* | 4–5 ft × 4 ft | Lower-tier. Stiff branches for grill skewers |

## Layout

- Anchors (pineapple guava, loquat) every ~8 ft
- Mid-tier (pomegranate, dwarf olive) in between
- Tea camellia + rosemary fill lower spaces
- 4–5 ft gaps for stepping-stone path
- 3–4 in mulch, irrigate from Phase 3 for 2–3 years
