## Planting band layout

- ~6 ft strip along each wall. East + west strips run full length down to the patio (T15 at south end of west band, T16 at south end of east band)
- Middle of south: lawn extends to patio (no planting band there)
- Cap depth in planting band: 16–24 in (deeper than lawn for tree roots)
- Cap depth in lawn: 12 in

## Tree-planting rules

1. **Trunk ≥ 3 ft from any wall.** Applies to all 4 walls equally.
2. **Wider, NOT deeper** planting holes (2–3× rootball wide, rootball depth). Do not break through cap into structural clay below (creates bathtub effect).
3. Use cap soil as backfill — don't add bagged amended mix.
4. **Mycorrhizal inoculant in every planting hole** (granular, 1 tsp).
5. Aggressive-root species (fig T9, mulberry T4, pomegranate T10) → place on lot-side edge of band, not tight to wall.
6. Drip emitters on lot side of trunk, not wall side (trains roots inward).
7. Stone fruits (T5, T6, T7): handful of pea gravel + sand in hole bottom for extra drainage.
8. Olive (T11): pinch of dolomitic lime in backfill (alkaline-preferring).
9. Citrus (T12, T13): pinch of elemental sulfur (mildly acidic-preferring).
10. Mulch right up to the wall is fine — trailing rosemary on wall cascade will overhang.
