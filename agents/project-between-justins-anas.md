# Project — Between Justin's & Ana's House

## Intent

Productive evergreen privacy screen between the two houses. Blocks direct line of sight. Pass-through walkable (clusters with 4–5 ft gaps + stepping stones, NOT a solid hedge).

## Conditions

Partial shade (between two house walls). Sunnier side gets the citrus + rosemary; shadier end gets tea camellia + strawberry tree.

## Plant palette

| Plant | Sci. | Mature size | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pineapple guava 'Coolidge' | *Feijoa sellowiana* | 8–12 ft × 6–8 ft | Anchor. Evergreen. Edible flowers + fruit. Self-fertile |
| Bay laurel | *Laurus nobilis* | 10–15 ft, narrow | Columnar evergreen. Culinary bay leaves |
| Strawberry tree 'Marina' | *Arbutus unedo* | 10–15 ft × 8–10 ft | Evergreen. Bell flowers + red fruit simultaneously in fall |
| Yuzu | *Citrus junos* | 8–10 ft | Cold-hardiest citrus. Evergreen. Fragrant zest. **Thorned — keep off path side** |
| Tea camellia | *Camellia sinensis* | 4–6 ft × 3–5 ft | Evergreen. Leaves for green/black tea |
| Upright rosemary 'Tuscan Blue' | *Salvia rosmarinus* | 4 ft × 4 ft | Lower-tier evergreen filler. Culinary |

## Layout

- Anchors (pineapple guava / strawberry tree) every ~8 ft
- Bay laurel + tea camellia interleaved
- Rosemary at cluster edges (lowest tier)
- 4–5 ft gaps between clusters for stepping-stone path
- Yuzu away from walking line
- 3–4 in wood chip mulch; irrigate from Phase 3 for 2–3 years to establish
